What We Do

We are an organization dedicated to the elaboration of the best coffee, likewise, we provide technical-productive, financial, agroindustrial and commercial services that allow the access of our small affiliated companies to the national and international market, through the manufacture of finished products and / or the export of your coffee.

For the elaboration we use the Arabica and Robusta beans. Arabica is the most popular variety and has a subtle and exquisite aromatic flavor; and the Robusta gives the extra touch of vitality to the product.
